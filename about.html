About Wordify

About the Project

Wordify is an AI-powered legal document assistant designed to simplify complex legal language into clear, understandable insights. It enables users to analyze contracts, detect risks, identify loopholes, and generate summaries efficiently.

The platform integrates AI capabilities to provide features like clause detection, risk scoring, future impact simulation, and legal interpretation, helping users make informed decisions quickly.

Our Vision

Our goal is to make legal systems more transparent, accessible, and user-friendly by leveraging artificial intelligence to bridge the gap between legal complexity and common understanding.
