About the Project

Verdictium is an AI-powered legal document assistant designed to simplify complex legal language into clear, understandable insights.

It enables users to analyze documents, detect risks, identify loopholes, generate summaries, ask document-specific questions, and explore the consequences of their intended decisions.

The goal is to make legal understanding more transparent, accessible, and action-oriented.

Core Capabilities

Summarization

Clear legal summaries in supported languages.

Loophole Finder

Surfacing weak, vague, or one-sided clauses.

AI Chat Assistant

Ask follow-up questions after analysis.

Decision Risk Analysis

Risk score, consequences, and mitigation plan.
